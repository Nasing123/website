About

Hello World - I’m Natascha!

Since I was a small child I loved painting and maths. Over the time this changed into a fascination for the fast pacing technology which is drastically changing our society - and art with it’s very reflective nature.
I have studied "Art and Multimedia", an interdisciplinary study on media art and IT, afterwards I choose to take a deeper look into IT studies.
Since that time I worked in different fields where I have strengthen my skills and expertise. I worked as a Video Editor, Graphic Designer with focus on UI, freelance Media-Artist and Software Developer. Never being afraid to use different tools from different domains and follow unexpected ways to realize the best outcome for the projects, I always remained curious to learn more.

Whenever possible I take time for travel. Whenever I travel I take my camera with me. It’s the interaction with people from different cultures, and the understanding that we are all unique - but same in our essence, that thrives my wish to explore further and to capture the never-ending beauty that surrounds as all (... not to forget a small tiny little need for adventure once in a while ;).
